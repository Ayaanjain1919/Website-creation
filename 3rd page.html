Penne Pasta

Penne is a type of pasta with cylinder-shaped pieces whichs end is cut at an angle. Penne pasta was invented by Giovanni Battista Capurro, a pasta maker from San Martino d'Albaro. He invented a machine that cut pasta diagonally and the pasta instantly became famous.
